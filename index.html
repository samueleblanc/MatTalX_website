About

MatTalX is an open source project developed to enable peers, friends, teachers and students to discuss mathematics, physics, chemistry or any other related subject efficiently on any platform. It works by converting LaTeX commands into text, therefore making it possible to write math symbols anywhere on the internet.
Our main goals are to provide the widest range of symbols possible, while being incredibly fast.
If you have any suggestions, we would greatly appreciate your comment!
