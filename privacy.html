Privacy policy



The only permission needed for MatTalX to work properly is "Storage". It is used to store your work in progress, if any. The text written in the first box (input) right before closing MatTalX is saved, so that you are able to change tab and continue the text you started without having to start over. The settings, namely 'Adjust spaces', 'Mathematical font' and 'Math mode', are also saved.

MatTalX does NOT have access to you browser history, to your open tabs or any other private information.
